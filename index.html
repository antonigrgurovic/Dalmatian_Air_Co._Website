Installation

Established in 1999, Dalmatian Air Co. proudly serves the greater tri-state area specializing in commercial HVAC and heating units. All new installations are company guaranteed for 10 years. Because our company is family-owned, we offer our customers competitive rates compared to larger air conditioning companies. Call today for a free estimate.

Maintenance

All new installations come with annual maintenance contracts to ensure your unit continues to run smoothly. Emergency maintenance calls, whether contracted or not, are serviced next day if possible (Sundays excluded). Maintenance includes regular cleaning of the unit, freon check, leak inspection, electrical check, and parts inspection.
